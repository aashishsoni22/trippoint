            Established in 2009, The Mata Raj Kaur Institute of Engineering and Technology was founded by the MRK
            Education Society and is approved by AICTE, New Delhi, Government of India, Directorate of Technical
            Education, Govt. of Haryana and affiliated to Maharishi Dayanand University, Rohtak/ Indira Gandhi
            University Meerpur, Rewar, Haryana.

            ​

            Instituted with the motive of imparting theoretical as well as practical knowledge in the field of technical
            education, the MRK College of Engineering and Technology offers a 4-year Bachelor of Engineering and
            Technology: (B. Tech.) Program in Computer Science and Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics and
            Communication Engineering, Fire Technology and Safety Engineering and Mechanical Engineering. The institute
            also offers a 2-year Master of Engineering and Technology: (M. Tech.) Program in M. Tech Computer Science
            and Engineering, M. Tech Electronics and Communication, M. Tech Electrical Engineering and M. Tech
            Mechanical Engineering. Other undergraduate programs that the MRK College offers are a 3-year Bachelor
            Business Administration (BBA) program and 3-year Bachelor Computer Applications (BCA) program.

            ​

            Apart from academics, the institute encourages co-curricular activities by putting together sports, cultural
            and technical events and organizes NSS camps as a part of its social responsibilities. Industrial visits are
            an important part of the curriculum and industry professionals impart knowledge with training and
            demonstrations under the Academic-Industry Interaction program. The students are constantly motivated to
            achieve their utmost potential and moulded for a dynamic industry.

            ​

            Spread out over a luscious green ground with a pleasant environment, the institute is set up in the NCR
            region of Haryana which is 10 km from NH-8. It is easily accessible and situated in the heart of the
            industrial area, which is 40 km from Gurgaon, 10 km from Bawal, 27 km from Bhiwadi and 30 km from Neemrana.

            ​

            The MRK institute has excellent infrastructure with an in-house library and well-equipped laboratory
            facilities. Spacious and ergonomically well-designed lecture halls with modern teaching aids and 24*7 power
            backup generators ensure a comfortable and un-interrupted teaching process. The institute takes proud in its
            sumptuous library with a rich collection of textbooks, reference books and multi-media. The dedicated
            faculty who is engaged in research and consultancy is committed to delivering an unmatchable learning
            experience.

            ​

            The CNC machine (production) is an exclusive feature of the mechanical department which makes MRK Institute,
            the only institute with a CNC in the respective region. The institute also has a language lab equipped with
            the latest software and a computer centre with multimedia tools. Intending to groom the students into
            overall professionals, the MRK institute also provides with soft skills training to improve their
            communication skills. The campus network is managed by proxy servers, FTP servers and based on Windows 2008
            server Enterprise. Internet connectivity in the centre is provided through a leased line and the institute
            has a Wi-Fi connection which provides 24*7 internet facilities through a dedicated line.
